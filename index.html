Our mission

Transform ideas & people into business.

Flexship aims to in bring together your carefully selected and talented intrapreneurs with disruptive business ideas. We support the strengthening of your organisation entrepreneurial mind- and skillset by providing training to your most promising intrapreneurial talents. Besides, we are able to advise on changes in organisational culture and process to support entrepreneurial activities within organisations.

Our solution will enable your organisation to kickstart internal seed-projects and manage them transparently. This will serve your strategic goals to turn systematically developed seed-projects into new business.

Main Features

Identify intrapreneurial talents

Flexship is the best way to uncover intrapreneurial talents inside your organisation. Our identification tool has been developed and empirically validated at Ludwig Maximilian Univerity in Munich, it's Entrepreneurship Center and with several reputable employers. You will be in the position to find internal and examine external high-potentials with entrepreneurial mindset and abilities. Uncover your hidden entrepreneurial potentials now!

Develop disruptive ideas

Flexship supports you to develop new business ideas within your organisation. Our idea management tool enables you to push idea generation by social and rewarding mechanisms. You will be in the position to collect, evaluate and kickoff the development of ideas and enrich them with required intrapreneurship team skillsets. Develop your new business ideas now!

Match ideas with talents

Flexship ensures that your selected ideas will be developed by the right people. Our matching algorithm will support you to internally find and engage intrapreneurial talents for your new business ideas and set up internal seed-project teams. Kickstart your seed-projects now!

Manage seed-projects transparently

Flexship can provide you with more transparency and oversight of your ongoing seed-projects. Our dashboard solution lets you keep track of and manage your seed-projects portfolio. Successfully steer your seed-project portfolio now!
