Mission

Transform ideas & people into business.

Flexship provides software tools, training and consulting in the field of corporate entrepreneuship. We aim to bring together your carefully selected intrapreneurial talents with your best business ideas. Our workshops will strengthen your employees entrepreneurial mind- and skillset. Besides, we consult on organisational culture and processes to support entrepreneurial activities within organisations.

Our solution will enable your organisation to kickstart internal seed-projects. This will serve your strategic goals to turn systematically developed seed-projects into new business.

Products

Identify intrapreneurial talents

Flexship is the best way to uncover intrapreneurial talents inside your organisation. Our identification tool has been developed and validated at LMU Munich, it's Entrepreneurship Center and with several reputable companies. You will be in the position to find internal and examine external high-potentials with entrepreneurial mindset and abilities. Uncover your hidden entrepreneurial potentials now!

Develop disruptive ideas

Flexship supports you to develop new business ideas within your organisation. With our idea management tool you will be in the position to collect, evaluate and kickoff the implementation of disruptive business ideas. Start to realize your new business ideas now!

Match ideas with talents

Flexship ensures that your selected ideas are realized by the right people. Our matching algorithm will help you to internally find and engage intrapreneurial talents for your new business ideas and set up internal seed-project teams. Kickstart your seed-projects now!
